Environment

We went here twice already and from what I saw, as you walk inside the doors the place looks clean. Employess greet you right away, we didn't have to wait at all.

What do they serve

They mostly serve curry and ramen. The menu was just right. It wasn't too much or too little. There was enough variety to choose from.
